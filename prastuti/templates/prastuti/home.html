Technical Extravaganza 2021

ABOUT

Greetings from Dept. of Electrical Engineering, IIT BHU.

We are indeed honoured to unveil this edition of the annual techno-management fest of Dept. of Electrical Engineering.

The fest is majorly seen as a fiesta that celebrates the advent and development of Science and Technology and brings in a stupendous footfall of nascent young minds. Hosted in the pioneering institute, the fest has grown over the years in stature and has made its impact. With the endeavour to make it a glory, it would feature an array of innovative events and thus reconstructing the era of technological renaissance, promoting the growth of research instincts amongst the literati of the institute. This year, participants from across the institute will come together to celebrate the essence of neoteric techno-innovations through a deluge of events.

Looking forward to your zealous participation with the assurance that you'll return a little more enriched and fulfilled.
